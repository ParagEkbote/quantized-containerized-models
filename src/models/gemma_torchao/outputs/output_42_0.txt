**1. What is Machine Learning? (The Big Picture)**

At its core, Machine Learning is about teaching computers to *learn* from data, *without* being explicitly programmed for every single task.  Instead of writing specific rules to tell a computer exactly what to do, you give it data and let it figure out the rules itself.
